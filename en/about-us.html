ABOUT LES-EXPO

Meeting in Istanbul on September 9-11, 2025!

LES-EXPO, the world's first and only specialized fair in the field of cargo engineering services, will be held at the Istanbul Expo Center between September 9-11, 2025. This giant organization, brought to life in cooperation with the Cargo Engineering Association (YMB) and KFA Fair Organization, brings together the leading companies of the sector from Europe, Asia and Africa, bringing together all components of the cargo engineering ecosystem under one roof.

Playing a critical role in sectors such as construction, energy, maritime, heavy industry and infrastructure, cargo engineering services contribute approximately $12 billion to the Turkish economy annually. LES-EXPO will bring together crane, machinery, vehicle, equipment and service providers and provide the opportunity to learn about the latest technologies and solutions in the sector.

The fair, which will be held in both indoor and outdoor areas at the Istanbul Expo Center, aims to increase the global cooperation of the cargo engineering sector by hosting visitors from three continents. Join! LES-EXPO to take your place in this important meeting that will shape the future of the cargo engineering sector

What is Load Engineering?

Load engineering is a multidisciplinary field that involves the safe, accurate and planned lifting, transportation and placement of loads of unusual size and weight. This process is not only realized with vehicles, but also with the planning, analysis and decisions of the expert engineers who manage these vehicles.

Although not formally defined, load engineering encompasses critical processes such as risk analysis, ground assessment, vehicle selection and application monitoring in heavy-duty transport operations by land, air, sea, rail, and sometimes even undersea.

With the change of technology, climate and projects, the variety of cargo transported is increasing, making the future role of cargo engineering specialists even more vital.
