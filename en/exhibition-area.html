Turkey's Largest Fairground

Total fair area 200,000 m2

The largest international exhibition center in Eurasia

Electronic parking system for 7500 vehicles (1 indoor and 2 outdoor giant parking lots)

STRONG INFRASTRUCTURE AND SUPERIOR SERVICE

It provides an infrastructure that will fully meet the energy, heating-cooling, air conditioning, telecommunication, water, air, compressed air requirements that will support all kinds of technological tools and systems. It responds to all needs immediately.

Its proximity to the ring roads, the metro passing through the center, the availability of very large parking areas, environmental arrangements and excellent infrastructure have made İFM one of the attractive centers. It is located in the same complex together with İDTM Business Blocks, WOW Hotels, WOW Congress Center.

PARKING AREA

The 7500-vehicle capacity car park, arranged according to the hall locations, provides participants and visitors with comfortable accommodation and security throughout the fair.
