Istanbul Fair Center (IFM)

Opposite Ataturk Airport
34149 Yeşilköy

Tel: (+90 212) 468 52 00
Fax: (+90 212) 465 59 59

E-mail: expo@ifm.com.tr
Web: www.ifm.com.tr

Thousands of companies meet new markets and opportunities through international fairs such as Cosmetics & Home Ingredients, which are held in the fair halls operated by the Istanbul World Trade Center, are considered to be among the world's largest fairs in their sectors and attract hundreds of thousands of visitors from home and abroad every year.

You can use the following transportation options to reach the Fairground from other parts of Istanbul.

Transportation Options to the Fairground

Metrobus

The fastest way to get to the fairground from Zincirlikuyu, Mecidiyeköy and Avcılar is by Metrobus. You can use the 34 A metrobus line, transfer from the Atatürk - Şirinevler stop to the Atatürk Airport (M1A) metro, get off at the World Trade Center/Istanbul Fair Center station and reach the fairground.

www.iett.gov.tr

Metro

The Yenikapı-Ataturk Airport metro line (M1A) passes through the Istanbul World Trade Center/Istanbul Fair Center complex. Transfers can be made to the Ataturk Airport metro line from the Zeytinburnu or Şirinevler metrobus stops.

metro.istanbul

Bus

You can reach Istanbul Fair Center by using HVİST-13 buses departing from Istanbul Airport and M1A Yenikapı-Atatürk Airport metro route from Ataköy-Şirinevler metro stop.

www.iett.gov.tr

Car

You can reach the fairground by road from 3 alternative arteries (E5, TEM and Coastal Road).

Marmaray

You can reach the fairground in 5 minutes by taxi at the Yeşilyurt stop using the Halkalı-Gebze Marmaray line.

https://marmaray.istanbul/
