LES-EXPO

LES-EXPO-Cargo Engineering Services Fair Meets You at Istanbul Fair Center on September 9-11!

Les-Expo 2025 brings together the latest developments, technologies and innovative solutions in the field of freight engineering services.

This fair, which will be held with the participation of expert companies and professionals in the field, will offer you a unique opportunity to discover the latest trends in the sector and revolutionary innovations in engineering services. The fair, where Load Engineering services will be exhibited in an area of ​​28 thousand square meters at the Istanbul Fair Center, will offer both visitors and participants a business-oriented experience where technology and trends are at the forefront.
