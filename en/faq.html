Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.) by Visitors

You can register as a visitor online from the "Online Ticket" section on the homepage of our website. You can also register at the entrance on the day of the event, but online registration will provide faster entry.

The entrance fee to our fair is 200 TL.

The fair is open to visitors between 10:00-18:00 on weekdays and 10:00-20:00 on weekends. Last entry is 1 hour before closing.

You can download the fair layout plan from the "Fair Area" section of our website. You can also get a free printed plan at the fair entrance.

You can access the participant list from the "Participant List" section of our website. The list is updated regularly.

The fair will be held at the Istanbul Fair Center. Address: Yeşilköy Istanbul, Fair Center, 34149 Bakırköy/İstanbul. You can find public transportation and private vehicle transportation information on our "Transportation" page.
